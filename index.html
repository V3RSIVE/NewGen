About NewGen Technologies

Founded in March 2025, NewGen Technologies is a futuristic industrial innovation firm headquartered in the United States. We are committed to delivering cutting-edge solutions in security, energy, infrastructure, and human performance, guided by our core values: Honesty, Integrity, Safety, Innovation, and Commitment.

Our mission is to empower communities and industries with advanced technologies, from wearable exosuits to scalable energy systems, creating a more connected, secure, and sustainable future.
